Cameronian Pancake

Cameroonian pancakes are soft,fluffy and absolutely delicious!! They look like crepes but are thicker and they are different from American pancakes. With only 8 ingredients and some simple steps, you get a yummy breakfast/travel meal for the entire family.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups flour (250 grams or 1/4 kg)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (100 grams)
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 cup liquid milk (240ml)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract substitute with half a teaspoon of ground nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons oil
